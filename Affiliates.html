Guyana Red Cross Society

The Guyana Red Cross is an organization dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable people. Our actions are guided by the 7 fundamental principles:

  • Humanity
  • Impartiality
  • Neutrality
  • Voluntary Service
  • Independence
  • Unity
  • Universality
